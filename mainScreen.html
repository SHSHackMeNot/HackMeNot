About Us

We are a group of high school students who are interested in educating the public about the growing dangers of cyber-hacking.

Our website hosts a multitude of resources such as a simulation to educate you on how to stay safe and a secure password generator.

We are focused on educating all people, but especially older people and younger children who can easily be tricked into giving up their data or can easily be hacked. Our simulation hosted by our very own Hacky, the thumb drive, aims to teach users how they are susceptible to attacks and how to prevent them. The password generator is useful to those who are looking to keep their password secure and meeting all requirements.
